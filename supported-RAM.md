Librebooted X200 is picky with memory sticks. Here is what I tested.

## Works

### 4GB
Crucial CT51264BF160B
### 2GB
Qimonda IMSH2GS13A1F1C-10F

## Doesn't work

### 4GB
Hynix HMT451S6AFR8A-PB
Kingston KVR1333D3SO/4GR
